Construction Management

Managing the complex aspects of a major construction project can be a difficult and time-consuming task. The Siddique and Sons Construction Management services offer customers with a comprehensive management of all aspects of the project, from conception to completion.

General Contracting

Siddique and Sons provides a full range of General Contracting services for every project. We have the capability to monitor the critical issues and handle every facet of the project, from large construction projects to small buildings.

Design / Build

Design-Build construction delivery method is appropriate for many projects. Siddique and Sons has developed its role as a design-build contractor. It provides the best economical design and solutions that fit the client’s needs, all within the allocated budget and maintenance of quality.

Turn Key

Siddique and Sons specializes in undertaking projects from A to Z (Turn-Key) adhering to the highest level of safety & quality in both materials supplied and work constructed, and committing itself to hand over the projects within the allocated time frame.

Welcome

By looking forward to tomorrow we can perform better today.” “SIDDIQUE AND SON’S people are our greatest asset.

Before founding SIDDIQUE AND SON’S in 2018, I had been an active member of the construction industry since the late 1980’s. In some ways, it seems like such a long journey I have taken to get to this point from where I started with literally just a single vehicle and a dream of doing big projects. But in other ways, it seems like the blink of an eye. I am proud of all the hard work we’ve done and all the loyal people I’ve had work for us to achieve great success in what I am sure is just the first phase in the life of SIDDIQUE AND SON’S.

We have worked hard and continue to work hard to win more and larger projects throughout the region. We, as a company, enjoy the work we do and are proud of the finished projects. Part of our mission is identified as doing projects that benefit the general public and doing the right job the right way. In that spirit, and as we look to the next coming years of corporate success and beyond, we will continue to strive to be an efficient, professional, safe and responsible member of the community in which we work.

Construction Director
